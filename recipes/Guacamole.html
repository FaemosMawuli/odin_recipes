Guacamole

Description

The above image is the famous Guacamole! In theory is just smooshed avocado but in practice is the bedrock of Mexican Culture. No joke!

Ingredients

  1. 3 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  2. 1 lime, juiced
  3. 1 teaspoon salt
  4. Â½ cup diced onion
  5. 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  6. 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  7. 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  8. 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (Optional)

Steps

  • In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt. Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper. Refrigerate 1 hour for best flavor, or serve immediately.
